Our Story

Welcome to "My Cupper" coffee shop, brought to life in 2025. We are not just baristas and business owners; we are parents and siblings who want to create a warm, welcoming extension of our own home right here in the heart of Beckenham.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide the best service possible and become a meaningful part of your everyday life. We source only the finest, ethically grown beans because we believe that quality is a form of respect.

We also believe a strong society is built on kindness, support, and togetherness. Through future community events, supporting local causes, and simply providing a space where everyone feels they belong, we hope to nourish Beckenham in ways that go far beyond the coffee.

So, come on in and let us be a part of your day. We are incredibly excited to share our passion with you, to learn your name, and to show you what our family is all about.
